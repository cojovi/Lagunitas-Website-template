Taking Things Apart

At Stargazer Project, we have a unique passion for taking apart old or unused electronics. We believe that there's a treasure trove of valuable peripherals hidden in what people consider to be "trash." These components can be repurposed and reused to create amazing and innovative solutions, breathing new life into discarded technology.

By salvaging and repurposing electronic components, we contribute to reducing electronic waste and promoting sustainable practices. Our mission is to explore the untapped potential of discarded electronics and unlock new possibilities through creativity and innovation.
